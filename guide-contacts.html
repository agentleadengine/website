Managing Your Contacts

Every lead, client, and prospect lives in your Contacts. Here’s how to search, view, and manage them.

CONTACT RECORDS

What You’ll See for Each Contact

Every contact record gives you a complete picture: details, history, and context in one place.

100%
Auto-Captured

Contact Details

Name, phone, email, address, and any custom fields specific to their insurance line. All captured automatically from form submissions.

6+
Smart Tags

Tags

Tags like ‘new-lead’, ‘policy-active’, ‘ready-to-buy’, ‘needs-follow-up’, and ‘referral’ are applied automatically by your workflows. They help you segment and filter your database.

7
Stages

Pipeline Stage

See exactly where each contact is in your pipeline, from New Lead through Closed Won. One glance tells you where they stand.

360
Full View

Activity History

Full timeline of every interaction: calls, texts, emails, form submissions, appointments, notes. Nothing gets lost.

Unlimited
Notes

Notes

Add notes to any contact after a call or meeting. Your team can see the full context before picking up the phone.

3
Channels

Communication

Call, text, or email any contact directly from the CRM. All communications are logged automatically in their activity history.

SEARCH & FILTER

Searching & Filtering

Find any contact instantly and take action on groups of contacts at once.

Search

Use the search bar to find any contact by name, phone number, or email. Results appear instantly. You can also use Smart Tags to filter by status, insurance line, or pipeline stage.

Bulk Actions

Need to tag 50 contacts at once? Export a segment? Send a mass message? The bulk actions menu lets you act on multiple contacts simultaneously.
