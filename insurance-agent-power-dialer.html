WHY MANUAL DIALING LOSES

Why Manual Dialing Loses

Manual dialing gets 8 to 12 calls per hour. Power dialing gets 25 to 40. If you are calling 300 leads a day, the dialer is the difference between quota and below quota.

Manual Dialing Is Slow

Dialing one number at a time caps you at 8 to 12 connects per hour. Half your day is dead ringing.

Disposition Tracking Fails

Writing dispositions in a notebook or spreadsheet is lost context by the next redial.

TCPA Cell Phone Risk

Auto dialers to cell phones without consent is the most common TCPA violation. Compliance critical.
