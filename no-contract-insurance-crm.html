WHAT LEGACY CRMS DO

What Legacy CRMs Do

The enterprise insurance CRM playbook has not changed in 15 years. Big implementation fees, long contracts, painful exits. If you are a solo or 2 person shop, you should never sign anything that takes longer to cancel than it took to sign up.

Annual Contracts

12 to 24 month terms are industry standard. You are locked in even if the product breaks or you outgrow it.

5 Figure Setup Fees

Salesforce Financial Services Cloud, AgencyBloc, and HubSpot all charge implementation fees in the $5,000 to $25,000 range.

Data Hostage Exits

Some CRMs charge extra to export your book of business when you leave. That is a ransom, not a contract.
