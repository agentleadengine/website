MEDSUPP AGENT REALITY

MedSupp Agent Reality

MedSupp has the longest client lifetime in insurance. A T65 client who stays with you pays commissions for 15 to 25 years. Lose them in year 2 to a rate shopper and you lost $5,000 to $15,000 in future commission. ALE keeps them on the book.

T65 Lead Timing

T65 leads are most reach able 3 months before turning 65. Miss the window and they are on an MA plan or locked into a competitor MedSupp.

Birthday Rule States

California, Oregon, and others have birthday rule rate shops. Without tracking, your clients get poached at the right moment.

Rate Increase Churn

Carriers push rate hikes annually. Without proactive rate comparison, your book is ripe for competitors every December.
