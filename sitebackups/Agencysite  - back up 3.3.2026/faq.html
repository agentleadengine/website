FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Straight Answers. No Marketing Fluff.

Real questions from agency owners like you, about pricing, websites, CRM, timelines, and guarantees.

General

What does Agent Lead Engine do?

We're a managed digital presence partner built exclusively for independent insurance agencies. We offer three core services: CRM & Automation, premium websites, and Local Presence Management. Bundled into three tiers (Launch, Build, Command). You choose the tier, we handle everything.

Why is this only for insurance agencies?

Insurance has specific categories, keywords, compliance requirements, and buyer behavior. A generic marketing agency wastes time learning your industry. We already know it. The templates are insurance-specific, the content is tailored, and the SEO strategy targets insurance shoppers. Specialization is our advantage.

Will this work for independent agents, captive agents, or both?

Both. Independent agencies get full control and flexibility. Captive agents have carrier guidelines to follow, but we optimize within those rules. Either way, you show up when prospects search for insurance locally.

Pricing & Packages

What are the three tiers?

  • Launch ($497/mo): Insurance CRM & Automation. Full CRM with pipelines, automated follow-up, missed call text-back, review requests, calendar booking, and 30-min onboarding call.
  • Build ($897/mo): Everything in Launch + a premium 11-page website from our template library, tailored content, SSL + hosting, CRM + website analytics.
  • Command ($1,397/mo): Everything in Build + full Local Presence Management: Google Business Profile optimization, 3 GMB posts/week, 5 citations/month, managed review responses, and monthly strategy calls.

Which tier is right for my agency?

Launch if you need a system to capture and follow up with leads. It's the fastest way to stop losing prospects. Build if you want a professional website plus CRM. This is our most popular tier. Command if you want the complete managed digital presence with CRM, website, and Local Presence Management. We'll recommend the right fit on your consultation call.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. Every tier is $0 setup, and your monthly fee is your total cost. No hidden charges. CRM, SSL, hosting, and all included services are bundled. Add-ons are optional and clearly priced.

Can I upgrade tiers later?

Yes. Many agencies start on Launch and upgrade to Build when they're ready for a professional website. Since every tier is month-to-month with no contracts and $0 upgrade fees, you can upgrade (or downgrade) at any time. Just let us know and we'll adjust your plan.

What about multi-location agencies?

Multi-location pricing applies to Build and Command tiers. Build: +$500/mo per additional location. Command: +$800/mo per additional location. CRM (Launch) handles multiple locations natively within a single sub-account.

Websites

How many templates do you have?

9 unique templates, each with its own design system with different layouts, typography, and color palettes. These aren't cookie-cutter themes with different colors. Each template is architecturally unique. Browse them here.

What pages are included?

Every website includes 11 pages: Homepage, Auto Insurance, Home Insurance, Business Insurance, Life Insurance, Motorcycle/Boat/RV, Blog, Blog Post Template, FAQ, Contact, and Terms of Service. Command tier gets up to 5 additional custom pages.

How long does it take to build my website?

Build tier: 5-7 business days after we receive your info. Command tier: 10-14 business days (more pages, more customization, plus Local Presence Management setup). We handle everything. You just provide your agency details and choose your template.

Do I own my website?

You own the content. The template license is included as part of your monthly service. If you cancel, you receive a static HTML export of your site. Hosting, updates, and managed services end with cancellation.

I already have a website. Do I still need this?

If your current site converts visitors into quote requests and looks professional, start with Launch (CRM only) and integrate your existing site's forms into the CRM pipeline. If your site is outdated, not mobile-friendly, or doesn't represent your agency well, Build gives you a complete website upgrade plus the CRM to capture every lead.

CRM & Automation

What CRM platform do you use?

Agent Lead Engine CRM, deployed from a proven insurance snapshot and configured specifically for insurance agencies. Pipelines, automations, forms, and calendar are pre-built and ready to use. You don't need to learn the platform yourself. One user license is included with Launch and Build; three with Command. Additional users are $100/mo per user.

What automations are included?

Full nurture sequence for new leads, missed call text-back, review request automation (after policy binding), birthday reminders, renewal reminders, and automated booking confirmations. Plus quote request, contact, and review forms that feed directly into your pipeline.

What if I cancel? Do I keep my CRM data?

Yes. You own all contacts and data. On cancellation, we export everything for you. Pipelines and automations are specific to the Agent Lead Engine CRM, but your contact data goes with you.

Guarantees & Expectations

How does the 30-day money-back guarantee work?

Simple: if you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of service on any tier, we refund your payment in full. No questions asked, no hoops to jump through.

What is the 20-Lead Guarantee?

Command tier includes our 20-Lead Guarantee: if you don't get at least 20 leads within your first 90 days, we keep working for free until you do. Local Presence Management is a longer-term investment, so we back it with a results-based guarantee. We're confident in our system, so we put our money where our mouth is.

Are there any contracts or commitments?

No. Every plan is month-to-month. No contracts, no minimum terms, no cancellation fees. You stay because the work speaks for itself, not because you're locked in. Cancel anytime with 30 days' notice.

How quickly will I see results?

CRM results are immediate. Your pipelines, automations, and lead capture forms are active from Day 1. You'll see leads in your pipeline within the first week. Website results follow quickly (site live in 5-14 days). Local Presence Management (Command tier) is a longer-term investment that compounds over months. Monthly reports show you exactly what's been done and how your metrics are tracking.
