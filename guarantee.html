THE TERMS

Four Things We Promise

Short version: no surprises. Long version below.

No Setup Fee

$0 to start on Launch, Build, and Command. You pay month one and we deploy. Every competitor we benchmarked charges between $500 and $4,000 to get started.

Month to Month

No annual commitment. No 6 month minimum. No 12 month minimum. You are not locked in and neither are we. This keeps us honest.

30 Day Satisfaction Refund

If inside your first 30 days you feel the deployment, onboarding, or automations did not deliver what we promised, email support and we refund month one.

Full Data Export On Offboarding

You own your contacts, your pipelines, your conversations, and your content. We export everything as CSV and PDF so you can move it anywhere.

UPGRADING TO COMMAND

How the Guarantee Works When You Upgrade

If you start on Launch or Build and later upgrade to Command, the 30 day satisfaction guarantee applies to the upgrade, not the full Command price.

You Only Risk the Difference

If you are on Build at $1,447 per month and upgrade to Command at $2,147 per month, your guaranteed refund covers the $500 difference between the two tiers. You already committed to Build and received that value. The guarantee protects you on the new services you are paying for, not the ones you were already using.

Same 30 Day Window

The 30 day clock starts on the day your Command upgrade goes live. If the local presence management, citation building, or other Command-tier services do not meet your expectations within that window, we refund the difference. You stay on your previous tier with no interruption.

SUPPORT

What Is Free and What Is Not

We want this to be clear before you sign up, not after.

Free: Anything We Broke

If something in your CRM is not working because of our setup, our automation, our deployment, or our code, we fix it at no charge. That is our responsibility and we do not bill for our mistakes. This applies for the entire duration of your service.

Paid: Anything You Changed

If you or your team made changes to the CRM and something stopped working, that falls under support credits. Support is billed at $75 per hour in 15 minute increments. One credit is $18.75 and the minimum for any support session is one credit. You only pay for what you use and you are never auto-billed for support you did not request.
