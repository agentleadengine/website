Enrollment Periods, Handled

Four enrollment windows, four automation modes. The CRM switches messaging and urgency based on the time of year.

AEP (Oct 15-Dec 7)

High volume intake, SOA capture, plan comparison tracking, enrollment confirmation workflows. SMS caps at compliant frequency.

OEP (Jan 1-Mar 31)

Plan change requests, dissatisfaction follow up for clients who want out of their AEP pick.

T65 Pipeline

Year round drip for people aging into Medicare, starting at age 64 and 6 months with IEP reminders.
