SAMPLE CALL AGENDA

What a Check-In Actually Looks Like

Not a status report. Not a pitch. A working session.

Your AM walks in with:

  1. Your pipeline snapshot — how many leads in each stage, what changed this week, any bottlenecks.
  2. Your AI Receptionist call log — sample transcripts, booked appointments, flagged calls that need your attention.
  3. Conversion numbers — lead → call → booked → closed at every stage, vs. last period.
  4. What they already did — automations they tuned, leads they imported, bugs they fixed, content they published.
  5. What they recommend next — one or two concrete moves for the coming period.

You bring:

  1. Any questions about how to use the platform.
  2. Anything you want built (campaign, form, report, automation).
  3. Anything that is not working (broken workflow, missed SMS, weird behavior).

You leave with a summary email and a punch-list of action items — both yours and theirs.
