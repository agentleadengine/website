Section 1: Overview

Purpose

This document standardizes what we deliver to every client every month,
ensuring consistent and professional service delivery across all
accounts.

Service Framework

- This document covers the recurring tasks performed for each client by
  tier

- All tasks have assigned frequencies: daily, weekly, monthly, or
  quarterly

- Service tiers include: Launch, Build, and Command

- Each tier builds upon the previous, with Launch as the foundation

Service Level Agreement

- All monthly deliverables completed by the 5th of the following month

- Clients receive timely reports and strategy recommendations

Section 2: Daily Tasks (All Tiers)

The following tasks are performed daily for all clients, regardless of
service tier.

Monitoring and Automation

- Monitor CRM for new leads and verify automations fired correctly

- Check that missed call text-back is sending (spot check 1-2 clients)

- Review internal notification emails for pipeline stage alerts

Client Support

- Respond to any client support requests within 4 business hours

- Document all support interactions in the ticketing system

Section 3: Weekly Tasks

Launch Tier

- Review CRM pipeline health for each client (stale leads, stuck deals)

- Verify all workflows remain published and active

- Check calendar booking functionality

Build Tier (In Addition to Launch)

- Monitor website uptime and performance

- Check form submissions are flowing into CRM correctly

- Review website analytics for anomalies

Command Tier (In Addition to Launch and Build)

Google Business Profile Content

- Publish 3 Google Business Profile posts (Monday, Wednesday, Friday)

- Post content guidelines: mix of tips, promotions, community, and
  seasonal content

- GMB post format: image + 150-300 word caption + CTA

Review Management

- Monitor and respond to new Google reviews within 24 hours

- Response guidelines: thank positive reviews by name, address negative
  reviews professionally and offer to resolve offline

Section 4: Monthly Tasks

Launch Tier

- Pull CRM dashboard metrics: new leads, closed won, closed lost,
  pipeline value

- Review automation performance (open rates, response rates)

- Identify stale leads and trigger re-engagement if needed

- Prepare monthly CRM summary for client

Build Tier (In Addition to Launch)

- Pull website analytics: traffic, page views, form submissions, bounce
  rate

- Update website content if needed (seasonal changes, new products)

- Check SSL certificate status

- Review and optimize CRM workflows based on lead data

- Prepare combined CRM + website report for client

Command Tier (In Addition to Launch and Build)

Citation Management

- Submit 5 citations to real directories

- Citation directories to use: Yelp, YellowPages, BBB, Bing Places,
  Apple Maps, Facebook Business, Nextdoor, Chamber of Commerce,
  InsuranceAgentDirectory, TrustedChoice

Google Business Profile Management

- Update Google Business Profile photos if new ones available

Local Presence Audit

- Run local presence audit using SEMRush Local

Comprehensive Reporting

- Compile full monthly report: CRM metrics + website analytics + local
  presence KPIs

- Include month-over-month comparisons and strategy notes

Client Strategy Call

- Conduct monthly strategy call with client (schedule in last week of
  month)

Section 5: Quarterly Tasks

Build Tier

- Conduct quarterly strategy call with client

- Full website content review and refresh

- CRM workflow optimization review

- Competitive analysis of client local market

Command Tier

- Deep local SEO audit

- Citation accuracy audit across all directories

- Review and update target keywords

- Competitor ranking comparison

- Recommend any tier upgrades or add-ons

Section 6: Reporting Templates and Guidelines

Launch Tier Report Contents

- Pipeline summary (new leads, won, lost, open)

- Pipeline value

- Automation performance metrics

- Recommendations

Build Tier Report Contents (In Addition to Launch)

- Website traffic summary

- Top pages by views

- Form submission count and conversion rate

- Website performance score

Command Tier Report Contents (In Addition to Launch and Build)

- Google Business Profile insights (views, searches, actions)

- Citation count and accuracy score

- Review count and average rating

- Local search ranking for target keywords

- GMB post engagement metrics

- Strategy notes and recommendations

Section 7: Quality Standards

Delivery and Timeliness

- All reports delivered by the 5th of the following month

- No client should go more than 30 days without a touchpoint

CRM Standards

- All CRM workflows must remain published at all times

Content Standards

- All GMB posts must be original, professional, and insurance-relevant

- Review responses posted within 24 hours

Performance Monitoring

- Escalation process: if a metric drops 20% or more month-over-month,
  flag for review

- Track all metrics against previous months for trend analysis
