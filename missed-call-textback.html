HOW IT WORKS

Four Steps. Runs By Itself.

Missed call text-back is a CRM automation that watches your business phone line. When a call comes in that you cannot answer, it takes over.

01

Call Comes In

Prospect dials your business number. If no one answers in the configured ring cycle, the CRM flags the missed call.

02

Auto-Text Fires

Within seconds, an SMS goes out to that caller with your agency name, an apology for the miss, and a link to book a callback.

03

Lead Drops Into CRM

The caller is added to your leads pipeline with source, timestamp, and phone number. No manual data entry.

04

Follow-Up Runs

If the caller does not reply, a 7-day nurture sequence keeps the lead warm with value-driven texts and emails until they book or opt out.

THE SMS SCRIPT

This Is What the Caller Sees

Short, human, compliant. Includes the agency name, a reason to reply, a booking link, and STOP opt-out language.

Default Missed Call Text-Back

"Hi, this is [Agency Name]. Sorry we missed your call. Were you looking for an insurance quote? Reply here or book a callback: [link]. Reply STOP to opt out."

After-Hours Variant

"Hi, this is [Agency Name]. We are closed right now but saw your call. A licensed agent will reach out first thing tomorrow. Need something urgent? Book a time here: [link]. Reply STOP to opt out."

Every text goes out through your dedicated CRM number with STOP handling active. The booking link routes into your CRM calendar with automated confirmations.
