CRM GUIDE

Calendar & Booking

Your CRM includes a booking calendar that lets prospects schedule appointments directly. Here’s how it works and how to manage it.

HOW BOOKING WORKS

How Booking Works

From discovery to confirmation, three simple steps.

01

Prospect Finds You

A prospect visits your website, clicks “Book a Consultation,” or receives a booking link in an automated message.

02

They Pick a Time

Your real-time calendar shows available slots. The prospect selects a time that works and fills in their details.

03

You Both Get Notified

The prospect receives an automated confirmation. Your team receives a Calendar Booking Alert with all the details. Reminders fire automatically before the appointment.

MANAGE YOUR CALENDAR

Managing Your Calendar

Control your availability and appointment types from one place.

Set Your Availability

Define your working hours, buffer time between appointments, and booking window. Only available slots show to prospects. Block off lunch, meetings, or personal time and it’s automatically reflected.

Appointment Types

Your calendar is configured with insurance-specific appointment types: quote consultations, policy reviews, claims discussions. Each type has its own duration and booking page.
