SMS DELIVERABILITY

Why Your Insurance Agency Texts Are Not Delivering

You send a text. The client never gets it. You don't get a bounce notice. Here are the 7 most common reasons insurance agency SMS messages disappear into the void, and exactly how to fix each one.

TOP 3 CAUSES

The Quick Wins

1. Unregistered 10DLC

Most common cause in 2026. US carriers filter all unregistered A2P traffic. Brand and campaign registration required. Fix: register both.

2. Throughput violation

You hit the per second message cap for your campaign tier. Overflow messages get dropped. Fix: upgrade campaign tier or throttle sends.

3. Content filtering

Words like "loan", "free trial", "claim", certain URLs, or shortened links trigger carrier filters. Fix: plain language, full domain URLs.

DIAGNOSIS

How to Actually Debug a Delivery Problem

1. Send test to your phone

Use a phone on a different carrier than your own. If you get it, carrier filter. If not, provider problem.

2. Check message logs

Your CRM has send logs. Look for status codes. "Delivered" vs "Failed" vs "Undelivered".

3. Check 10DLC status

Log into your provider dashboard. Brand status should be "Verified." Campaign status should be "Approved."

4. Run a content test

Send the same message content via a different channel. Swap URLs, remove trigger words, retry.
