AEP PLAYBOOK

AEP Week by Week Playbook for Medicare Agents

Eight weeks of Medicare AEP broken down week by week. What to do, what to automate, and which numbers to watch so nothing slips between Oct 15 and Dec 7.

WEEK 0

Pre AEP Prep, Sep 15 to Oct 14

AEP is won or lost before it starts. The agents who coast through October and November are the ones who front loaded SOAs, booked review calls, and rehearsed their formulary talk tracks in September.

Book review slots

Every active client on the calendar before Oct 15. SOA link included in the booking confirmation.

Rehearse talk tracks

Script for formulary drops, premium jumps, MOOP changes, and carrier exits. Record yourself and listen back.

Stage the pipeline

Every client flagged AEP Outreach. SOA status, preferred channel, last touch date visible at a glance.
