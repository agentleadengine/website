T65 Lead Nurture Playbook for Medicare Agents

Turning 65 is the one moment in a lifetime when a consumer has to pick a Medicare plan. Whoever earns their trust first usually earns their book for the next 15 years. Here is the nurture sequence that wins that first appointment.

THE WINDOW

T65 Is an 18 Month Opportunity

The Initial Enrollment Period starts 3 months before the 65th birthday month and ends 3 months after. But the real nurture window is much longer. Prospects start researching Medicare 12 to 18 months before their birthday. If your first touch is at month 3, you are already losing.

Month 18 to 13, awareness

They are googling "how does Medicare work." Educational content wins here, not sales pitches.

Month 12 to 7, comparison

They are asking friends and family. Reviews, testimonials, and trust signals matter most.

Month 6 to 0, decision

Side by side plan compares. Dual eligibility checks. The appointment that closes.

THE SEQUENCE

The 18 Month Nurture Sequence

Every touch here is compliance safe: no unsolicited Medicare Advantage pitch, no plan comparisons outside of a signed SOA, and no discussion of benefits until the prospect requests it.
