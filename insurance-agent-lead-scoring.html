All Leads Are Not Equal

A web lead at 7 AM is hotter than one that opted out of SMS twice. Without scoring, producers waste cycles on the wrong leads.

First Come First Serve Fails

No Engagement Tracking

Email opens, SMS replies, and page views all signal interest. Without tracking, signals are lost.

Follow Up Gets Random
