RATER INTEGRATIONS

Your Raters, Talking To Your CRM.

Most insurance CRMs do not ship with rater connectors. We build the bridges between EZLynx, QuoteRush, Quotit, TurboRater, PLRater, and the Agent Lead Engine CRM so quote data lands in the contact record and fires follow up automation automatically.

THE PROBLEM

Double Entry Kills Solo Producers

Quote a lead in EZLynx, then re enter the name, phone, email, coverage, and premium into your CRM to trigger follow up. 40 leads a week, 20 minutes of duplication a day. Thats 40 hours a quarter you could be selling.

Quote To CRM

Every quote generated in your rater lands automatically as a contact in your CRM with premium, carrier, and coverage fields populated.

Status Sync

Bind in rater, pipeline stage in CRM updates to Placed. Cancel in rater, CRM flags for re engagement.

Automation Triggers

Quote sent fires a 5 touch SMS and email sequence. Bound fires a review ask. Lost fires a win back.
