THE 5 MODULES

One Module Per Insurance Line You Write.

Each module is a full CRM snapshot — not a template. Pipeline stages match the actual sales cycle, custom fields capture the underwriting data, and automations fire at the right moments.

🏠 Personal Lines

Auto · Home · Umbrella · Renters

Pipeline stages: New Lead → Contact Made → Quoting → Quote Sent → Follow-Up → Closed Won / Lost

Fields include: current carrier, renewal date, drivers, vehicles, dwelling details, prior claims, coverage limits, credit band.

Automations: quote-sent follow-up sequence, renewal reminders 60/30/15 days out, multi-line cross-sell after close.

🏢 Commercial Lines

BOP · Workers Comp · GL · Professional Liability

Pipeline stages: New Lead → Discovery Call → Gathering Info → Quoting → Proposal Sent → Negotiation → Closed Won / Lost

Fields include: industry code (NAICS/SIC), payroll, revenue, prior losses, exposures, policy year, broker of record status.

Automations: ACORD request follow-up, proposal nudges, BOR letter tracking, renewal 90-day sequence.

❤️ Life Insurance

Term · Whole · Universal · Indexed

Pipeline stages: New Lead → Contact Made → Needs Analysis → Application Started → Underwriting → Policy Issued → Closed Won / Lost

Fields include: beneficiaries, face amount, premium, health class, medical exam status, product type, carrier.

Automations: needs-analysis questionnaire, underwriting stall check-ins, medical exam reminders, policy delivery confirmation.

🏥 Medicare & ACA

MA · MAPD · Part D · PDP · ACA Marketplace

Pipeline stages: New Lead → Contact Made → Eligibility Check → Plan Comparison → Enrollment → Confirmation → Active Policy / Closed Lost

Fields include: date of birth, SEP/AEP status, current plan, Rx list, preferred providers, subsidy eligibility, effective date.

Automations: AEP campaign sequences (Oct–Dec), eligibility verification, SOA delivery, enrollment confirmation, retention outreach.

🕊️ Final Expense

Burial Insurance · Small Whole Life · Pre-Need

Pipeline stages: New Lead → Contact Made → Needs Assessment → Presentation → Application → Underwriting → Policy Issued → Closed Won / Lost

Fields include: age, health conditions, medications, coverage amount, beneficiary, payment method, appointment set.

Automations: appointment confirmation sequences, no-show recovery, simplified-issue routing, application follow-through.
