NORTH CAROLINA REALITY

North Carolina Reality

NC has three distinct markets: coastal hurricane, mountain ice storm, and triangle metro growth. Without a market specific workflow, you miss the placement nuance and the retention opportunities.

Hurricane Churn

Coastal counties see 1 to 3 named storms per season. Claim volume drives carrier non renewals and book churn.

Beach Plan Placement

NC JUA and IBHS windpool placement is complex. Deadlines and inspection requirements are easy to miss.

Triangle Metro Growth

Raleigh, Durham, Chapel Hill metro is high growth. Realtor partner tracking captures the volume.
