SECTION 1: PRE-ONBOARDING

> Complete these tasks before the onboarding call:

Confirm client\'s selected tier (Launch, Build, or Command)

Collect payment and confirm first invoice

Send welcome email with what to expect and timeline

Schedule onboarding call (30 min for Launch, 60 min for Build/Command)

Create client sub-account in GHL using appropriate snapshot

Deploy CRM snapshot to new sub-account

Assign team member at agency level (required for calendars)

Verify all workflows are published in the new sub-account

SECTION 2: INFORMATION TO COLLECT FROM CLIENT

☐ Contact person name and email

☐ Lines of insurance offered

☐ Service area / territory

☐ Business hours

☐ Existing website URL (if any)

☐ Google Business Profile access (email/password or admin invite)

Build and Command Tiers

☐ Template preference (from 9 options: Beacon, Apex, Crest, etc.)

☐ Agency logo (high resolution)

☐ Headshot photos of agents/team

☐ Office photos (if available)

☐ Preferred color scheme or brand colors

☐ Tagline or unique selling proposition

☐ Client testimonials or reviews to feature

☐ Specific content requests or messaging preferences

☐ Domain name (existing or new)

Command Tier

☐ Google Business Profile login credentials

☐ List of current citations/directory listings

☐ Target keywords for local SEO

☐ Competitor agencies to monitor

☐ Any existing review management tools

SECTION 4: POST-ONBOARDING SETUP

Launch Tier

Verify all automations are firing correctly (send test lead)

Confirm calendar booking URL works

Send client their CRM login credentials

Send client their calendar booking link

Schedule first check-in (2 weeks after go-live)

Build Tier (add these)

Begin website build using selected template

Write tailored content with local keywords

Integrate all forms with CRM pipeline

Client review round (Day 5-7)

Deploy website and verify SSL

Send client their website URL

Schedule quarterly strategy call

Command Tier (add these)

Run 12-point Google Business Profile optimization

Set up SEMRush Local

Submit first 5 citations

Create first week of GMB posts

Set up review link and response monitoring

Generate baseline local presence report

Schedule monthly strategy call

SECTION 5: GO-LIVE CHECKLIST

☐ All CRM workflows published and tested

☐ Website live and SSL active (Build/Command)

☐ All forms submitting to correct pipeline (Build/Command)

☐ Google Business Profile optimized (Command)

☐ First GMB posts scheduled (Command)

☐ Client has received all login credentials

☐ Client has received all booking URLs
