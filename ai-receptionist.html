WHAT IT DOES

Five Jobs, Done Every Time

The AI receptionist is not a chatbot pretending to be a person. It is a structured voice agent built around the jobs an insurance receptionist actually does.

Answers the Call

Picks up on the first ring, any hour, any day. Identifies the agency and asks how it can help. No menu trees.

Qualifies the Lead

Asks about line of business, current coverage, rough premium, state, and timeline. Captures the answers as structured CRM fields.

Books the Appointment

Reads the live calendar, offers real slots, and books the meeting with automated confirmations and reminders.

Live Transfers Hot Leads

If an agent is available and the lead is qualified and urgent, it warm transfers the call with a summary in your ear.

Logs Everything to CRM

Full transcript, recording, qualification data, and pipeline entry drop into your ALE CRM as soon as the call ends.

Follows Up Automatically

If the call does not close with a booking, it triggers the standard SMS nurture sequence so the lead stays warm.

SAMPLE CALL FLOW

What the Caller Hears

Every script is customized to your agency voice, but here is the default skeleton.

Opening

"Thanks for calling [Agency Name], this is the virtual assistant. I can help you with a quote, a policy question, or getting you scheduled with an agent. What can I help you with today?"

Qualification

"Got it, you are looking for a home and auto quote. Are you currently covered somewhere else, or is this a new policy? And what state are you looking to insure in?"

Booking or Transfer

"I can get you on the calendar with a licensed agent. I have Thursday at 10am or Friday at 2pm, or I can transfer you right now since an agent is available. What works best?"

Every call ends with confirmation of next steps and a summary SMS sent to the caller.

LIMITATIONS

What We Will Not Pretend It Does

Honesty matters more than hype. The AI receptionist is great at a specific range of tasks. Here is where you still want a human.

Complex Claims

It will capture the claim detail and escalate. It will not adjudicate or commit to coverage decisions.

Binding Policies

Quoting and binding stays with licensed agents. The AI books the appointment, the human writes the policy.

Sensitive Conversations

Bereavement, angry escalations, or anything requiring judgment gets transferred to a live agent immediately.
