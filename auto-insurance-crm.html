AUTO RENEWAL CHURN

Auto Renewal Churn

Auto has the shortest renewal cycle in personal lines and the highest rate shopping behavior. 20 to 30 percent of auto clients shop every renewal. Without a renewal pipeline, you lose 1 in 4 policies a year.

Auto X Date Misses

Most agents touch 30 to 40 percent of auto renewals late. That is when rate increases trigger rate shops.

Rate Shop Response Time

Client calls for a rate review, you call back 2 days later. They already called the agent down the street. Renewal lost.

Home Bundle Not Offered

Monoline auto clients have a home elsewhere. 20 to 30 percent would bundle if asked. Most never get asked.
