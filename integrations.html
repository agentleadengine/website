INTEGRATIONS

Connects To What You Already Use.

Agent Lead Engine runs on the GoHighLevel platform, which means native integrations with the tools most insurance agencies already rely on, plus a flexible Zapier and webhooks layer for everything else. Here is exactly what is supported, what needs custom work, and what is not there yet.

NATIVE INTEGRATIONS

Works Out of the Box

These are built into the ALE CRM snapshot. No custom work, no code, just connect your account and go.

Gmail

Two way email sync, send and receive from your Gmail right inside the CRM conversation feed.

Outlook

Two way email sync with Microsoft 365 and Outlook.com accounts.

Google Calendar

Native calendar booking with real time availability, auto confirmations, and reminder automations.

Microsoft Calendar

Same booking integration against Outlook and Microsoft 365 calendars.

Facebook Lead Ads

Instant capture of every Facebook lead form submission into your leads pipeline with SMS and email nurture.

Instagram DMs

Unified conversation feed shows DMs alongside SMS, email, and call history.

Google Business Profile

Respond to GBP messages from the CRM inbox, post updates, track call and direction actions.

Stripe

Accept payments for policies, down payments, or agency fees. Payment links embed in emails and SMS.

QuickBooks

Sync contacts and invoices with QuickBooks Online.

Zoom

Auto generate Zoom links for every booked appointment.

Zapier

Zapier trigger and action support for contacts, opportunities, and appointments. Opens up 5,000+ other apps.

Webhooks

Inbound and outbound webhooks for custom integrations your developer can build in an hour.

AMS SYSTEMS

Agency Management System Compatibility

Your AMS is your system of record for policy data. ALE is your system of engagement for leads and follow up. Most agencies run both. Here is the integration status.

AMSStatusHow It Works
EZLynxVia Zapier or webhooksPush new CRM contacts into EZLynx on policy bind. Pull renewal dates for ALE reminder automations.
HawkSoftVia Zapier or webhooksSimilar bidirectional sync through their API endpoints.
Applied EpicCustom webhook workEnterprise connector available for larger agencies. Scoped as a custom project.
AMS360 (Vertafore)Custom webhook workScoped integration using Vertafore API. Most useful for Command tier agencies.
NowCertsVia ZapierStandard Zapier app with contact and policy triggers.
QQCatalystVia Zapier or CSV syncScheduled contact import plus manual push on bind.
Applied TAMCustom work requiredLegacy platform, integration done as a project.
JenesisVia ZapierContact and policy sync supported.
CARRIER AND RATER TOOLS

Quoting and Lead Sources

These are the tools agents already use to generate leads and quotes. Integration is usually a Zap or a webhook.

PL Rater

Capture quotes into CRM as opportunities. Trigger follow up automations on quote completion.

TurboRater

Inbound webhook captures rate comparisons directly into the lead pipeline.

EZLynx Rating

Via EZLynx integration layer above.

QuoteWizard, MediaAlpha, EverQuote

Standard lead vendor delivery via email parsing, ping post, or direct API.

SmartFinancial, Datalot, NextGen

Lead vendor integrations via standard delivery methods.

Carrier Portals

Carrier portals themselves are not directly integrated. Quoting stays in the carrier tool, CRM tracks the lead and follow up.

NOT YET SUPPORTED

What We Do Not Pretend To Do

Being honest about gaps saves you a painful surprise later.

Deep AMS Sync

Real time bidirectional sync of every policy field to every AMS is not realistic without a custom build per AMS. Zapier and webhooks cover the common cases.

Carrier API Binding

We do not bind policies directly through carrier APIs. Binding happens in your existing rater or carrier portal.

Accounting Full Sync

QuickBooks contact sync works. General ledger and commission reconciliation is out of scope.
