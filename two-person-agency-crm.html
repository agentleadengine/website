THE 2 PERSON PROBLEM

The 2 Person Problem

2 person agencies have the hardest CRM decision. You need shared visibility, role based tasks, and renewal workflows, but you cannot afford a $25,000 implementation or a 24 month contract. Most 2 person shops run on sticky notes plus email plus memory.

Who Called Back?

Lead comes in, both of you think the other called. Lead churns to a competitor by the time you figure it out.

Who Owns The Renewal?

Producer thinks service rep is quoting. Service rep thinks producer owns renewal. Renewal goes to market unquoted.

Who Asked For The Review?

Client bound last week. Neither of you sent the review ask. Google rank stalls out at 12 reviews forever.

ROLE BASED WORKFLOW

Role Based Workflow

01

Shared Pipeline Visibility

Both of you see every new lead, every stage, every task. No more guessing who is on which deal.

02

Owner Tagging

Every lead gets assigned to producer or service rep at intake. Round robin or skill based routing, your call.

03

Role Based Automation

Producer gets quote task, service rep gets document request, both automated. No manual handoffs.

04

Renewal Handoff

X date triggers quote request to producer, service rep gets pending policy doc task. Everyone knows what to do.

05

Review Ask Owner

Whoever bound the client gets the review ask task automatically 7 days post bind. One owner, one ask, no double texts.

06

Daily Huddle Dashboard

Both of you open the dashboard at 8 AM. See who owes what, who is blocked, what is at risk. 5 minute standup, done.
