COMMERCIAL LINES PAIN

Commercial Lines Pain

Commercial accounts are high revenue, high touch, and high churn risk. Miss a COI request by a day, lose the account. Miss an annual review, the broker calls. ALE runs the service discipline that keeps accounts on the books.

COI Requests Pile Up

One mid market account sends 8 to 12 COI requests per year. Without an automated queue, they sit in email and damage the relationship.

RFP Process Is Chaotic

Marketing a new commercial account touches 4 to 8 carriers. Tracking responses in spreadsheets means missed submissions and stale quotes.

Annual Reviews Skipped

90 percent of commercial agents intend to do annual account reviews. Less than 30 percent actually run them. That is where churn starts.

COMMERCIAL AUTOMATION STACK

Commercial Automation Stack

01

RFP Pipeline

New submission triggers carrier routing, response logging, and quote comparison stage. Client sees progress, you close faster.

02

COI Request Queue

Every COI request enters a same day turnaround queue with a task for the service rep and a status update SMS to the requestor.

03

Certificate Renewal Tracker

Every policy COI expiration is tracked. Pre renewal reminders fire 30 days out. Clients never get a lapsed cert.

04

Annual Account Review

Policy anniversary triggers a review meeting request. Agenda template, exposure change questionnaire, quote opportunity log.

05

Audit Tracking

Workers comp and GL audits get tracked per policy. Reminder fires 60 days pre audit to prep the client.

06

Claims Retention Touch

Commercial claim opens trigger a check in call task, claims coordinator assignment, and retention touchpoints at 30 and 90 days.
