MI AGENT FAQ

Questions Michigan Agents Ask Us

Does the CRM account for Michigan's no-fault auto insurance reforms?

Our CRM captures lead information, automates follow-up, and organizes your pipeline. Quote details and PIP coverage selection stay with you and your carriers. The CRM simply ensures no lead goes cold while you work through options.

How does this help when Michigan has the highest auto rates in the country?

High rates mean more shopping. Speed-to-lead and consistent follow-up are the difference between winning and losing a quote request. Our automations make sure you're first to respond every time.

Can you segment Michigan-specific lead types in the pipeline?

Yes. Pipelines can be configured for auto, home, life, commercial, and specialty lines, with tagging for geography, coverage type, and lead source so you always know the next step.

Do you work with both Detroit-area brokerages and rural MI agents?

Yes. The setup is identical whether you're in downtown Detroit or a rural Upper Peninsula town. Everything is delivered and supported remotely.
