THE HEALTH AGENT REALITY

The Health Agent Reality

Health agents get hit with 80 percent of their year in 12 weeks. Leads are coming in 50 a day during OEP and AEP. If your pipeline is not automated and your SOA tracking is not bulletproof, you either miss enrollment windows or get a CMS audit flag.

OEP AEP Lead Volume

50 inbound leads a day during peak. You cannot call them all in 60 seconds without automation. Speed to lead drops, close rate collapses.

SOA Compliance

CMS requires scope of appointment for every Medicare lead, 48 hours before the appointment. Miss one and the whole enrollment is at risk.

Group Renewal Cycles

Small group benefits renew on different anchor dates. Without a renewal pipeline by anchor month, you miss the quoting window by weeks.

OEP AEP Pipeline
