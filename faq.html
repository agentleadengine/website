General Questions

What is Google My Business and why does my insurance agency need it?

GMB is the free Google tool that controls how your agency shows up in Google Search and Maps. When someone searches "car insurance near me," your GMB profile is what they see. Without it optimized, they're calling your competitor instead. Simple as that.

How is GMB optimization different for insurance agencies?

Insurance has specific categories (auto, home, life, commercial), compliance rules, and keywords that matter. We know exactly what works for insurance agencies because that's ALL we do. No guessing, no generic tactics—just insurance-focused optimization.

Will this work for independent agents, captive agents, or both?

Both. Independent agencies get full control and flexibility. Captive agents have to follow carrier guidelines, but we can still optimize your local presence within those rules. Either way, you get found.
