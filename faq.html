FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Your Questions, Answered Honestly

No marketing fluff. Straightforward answers about our services, pricing, websites, CRM, and guarantees.

General

What does Agent Lead Engine do?

We help insurance agencies get more leads with a CRM, a website, and Google search tools. You pick a plan. We set it all up and run it for you.

Why is this only for insurance agencies?

Because insurance is all we do. We already know the keywords, the customers, and what works. A general marketing company has to learn your business first. We don't. That saves you time and money.

Will this work for independent agents, captive agents, or both?

Both. We work with independent and captive agents. Either way, you show up when people search for insurance in your area.

Pricing & Packages

What are the three tiers?

  • Launch ($497/mo): CRM, automations, and a 5-page website. You get pipelines, lead follow-up, missed call text-back, review requests, and a booking calendar.
  • Build ($897/mo): Everything in Launch plus a full 11-page website. You own the site after 12 months.
  • Command ($1,397/mo): Everything in Build plus Google search dominance. We manage your Google profile, build citations, handle reviews, and guarantee you 20 leads in 90 days.

Which tier is right for my agency?

Launch if you want a CRM and a basic site to start getting leads now. Build if you want a full website you own after 12 months. Most popular. Command if you want the whole package plus Google search dominance. We help you pick on a free call.

Are there any hidden fees?

No. Zero setup fees. Your monthly price is your total price. Everything is included. Add-ons are optional and clearly priced.

Can I upgrade tiers later?

Yes. No contracts, so you can move up or down anytime. Many agencies start on Launch and upgrade to Build once they see results.

What about multi-location agencies?

Each location gets its own website and setup. Extra locations cost less: Build +$500/mo, Command +$700/mo per location.

Websites

How many templates do you have?

9 templates. Each one looks completely different. Not just color swaps. Different layouts, fonts, and styles. Browse them here.

What pages are included?

11 pages: Homepage, Auto, Home, Business, Life, Motorcycle/Boat/RV, Blog, Blog Post Template, FAQ, Contact, and Terms of Service. Command tier gets up to 5 extra pages.

How long does it take to build my website?

Build: about 7 business days. Command: about 14 business days. You pick a template and send us your info. We do the rest.

Do I own my website?

On Build and Command, you own the website after 12 months. On Launch, you get a copy of your content if you cancel. Hosting and updates are included in your monthly price.

I already have a website. Do I still need this?

Launch comes with a 5-page site that captures leads. If you want a bigger, better site you own after 12 months, go with Build.

CRM & Automation

What CRM platform do you use?

Our own CRM built for insurance agencies. It comes on every plan. We set up everything for you and keep it running. You don't need to learn it yourself.

What automations are included?

Every plan includes: automatic follow-up for new leads, missed call text-back, review requests after you close a deal, birthday messages, renewal reminders, and booking confirmations. All leads go straight into your pipeline.

What if I cancel? Do I keep my CRM data?

Yes. Your contacts and data are yours. If you cancel, we send you everything. Your data goes with you.

Guarantees & Results

How does the 30-day money back guarantee work?

If you're not happy in the first 30 days, you get all your money back. No questions asked. Zero risk to try.

Are there any contracts or commitments?

No contracts. Month-to-month. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice. You stay because it works, not because you're locked in.

What is the 20-Lead Guarantee?

Command tier only. If you don't get 20 leads in 90 days, we keep working for free until you do.

How quickly will I see results?

Your CRM and automations work from Day 1. Website goes live in 1-2 weeks. Google search results improve in 60-90 days. Results get stronger every month.
