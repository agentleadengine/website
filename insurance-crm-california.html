CA AGENT FAQ

Questions California Agents Ask Us

Does the CRM work for California's wildfire and earthquake disclosure workflows?

Yes. Quote forms can be configured to capture California-specific disclosures and risk details, and your pipeline can route leads by coverage type (fire, earthquake, flood, standard HO) automatically.

Can your system send bilingual follow-ups for Spanish-speaking California leads?

Yes. Automations can be configured to send bilingual SMS and email templates, and your CRM pipeline can tag leads by preferred language so you follow up accordingly.

We write across multiple California cities. Does pricing change by location?

The base tier covers a single agency location. If you need additional local search profiles or separate city websites, multi-location pricing starts at $500/mo per additional location.

Does this replace our California Department of Insurance compliance responsibilities?

No. Agent Lead Engine handles lead capture, CRM, automation, and local SEO. You and your agency remain responsible for CDI licensing, quote accuracy, and any state-level compliance requirements.
