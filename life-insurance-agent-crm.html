THE LIFE SALES CYCLE

The Life Sales Cycle

Most CRMs built for P&C break when you try to run life. Underwriting pendings sit in your inbox for weeks. Policy anniversaries disappear. Cross sell from term to whole never happens. ALE ships with a life specific pipeline out of the box.

Underwriting Stalls

30 percent of submitted life apps stall waiting on APS, exam, or lab work. Without a pending requirement tracker, they fall out.

Policy Anniversary Gap

Life policies renew annually but rarely get a touch. Beneficiary reviews, term conversion windows, IUL overloan risk all get missed.

Term To Whole Cross Sell

Client bought 20 year term at age 35. Best whole life conversion window is years 5 to 10. Most agents miss it entirely.

LIFE AGENT AUTOMATION STACK

Life Agent Automation Stack

01

Life Pipeline Stages

Quoted, Application, Underwriting, Approved, Issued, In Force. Every stage has a typical aging threshold and a reminder.

02

Requirement Tracker

APS, exam, lab, financial underwriting all tracked per case. Auto text to client when a requirement comes in late.

03

Policy Anniversary Touch

Every in force policy triggers a 30 days pre anniversary email and a beneficiary review SMS. Retention jumps.

04

Term Conversion Window

Term policies fire a conversion window task at year 5 and year 10 of the policy. Warm leads inside your own book.

05

Referral Ask Post Issue

3 days after policy issue, a congrats SMS asks for a referral. Life clients refer family and financial advisors.

06

AML And Replacement Compliance

AML forms, 1035 exchange tracking, and replacement disclosure logging built in. Stay audit ready.
