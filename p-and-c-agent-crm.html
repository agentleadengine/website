THE P&C REALITY

The P&C Reality

P&C has the tightest renewal cycle in insurance. 6 month auto, 12 month home, annual commercial. Your book is constantly expiring. If you are not running a renewal pipeline in a CRM, you are losing 15 to 25 percent of your book per year to rate shopping.

Auto 6 Month X Dates

Auto renews twice a year. Without X date automation, you touch 30 to 40 percent of renewals late, right when the rate increase hits.

Bundle Opportunities Missed

Monoline auto client has a home policy elsewhere. Without cross sell triggers, you never quote it. 20 to 30 percent bundle lift gone.

Claims To Retention Gap

Client files a claim, has a rough experience, shops their policy. Without a retention touch, you learn about it when the BOR letter arrives.

P&C AUTOMATION STACK

P&C Automation Stack

01

X Date Tracker

Every auto and home policy X date is tracked. 60, 30, 14, and 7 day reminders fire. No renewal is late ever again.

02

Bundle Cross Sell

Monoline auto? Auto bundle quote gets queued 30 days before renewal. Monoline home? Same. 20 percent bundle lift is typical.

03

Carrier Rater Push

PL Rater, EZLynx, QuoteRush, and ITC push quotes directly into the lead record. Stop double entering data.

04

Claims Retention Touch

Claim opened triggers a 7 day check in call task, 30 day retention touch, and 60 day satisfaction survey. Catches churn early.

Rate Shopper Detection

Client opens multiple renewal emails, clicks rate comparison links, or calls twice in a week, flag them for a retention call.

06

Referral Ask On Bind

P&C clients refer heavy. Post bind SMS asks for a referral. 2 to 3 referrals per month from a 300 household book is normal.
