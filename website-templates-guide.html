TEMPLATE SELECTION QUIZ

Find Your Template in 4 Questions

Pick the answer that feels closest. The templates grouped under each result fit that profile best.

1. What type of buyer do you serve most?

  • Families and homeowners: Clearwater, Heritage, Pacific
  • Business owners / commercial: Beacon, Apex, Corporate Pro
  • First-time buyers / younger: Vivo, Crest, Pacific
  • High-net-worth individuals: Beacon, Keystone, Corporate Pro

2. How old is your agency?

  • 20+ years, generational: Heritage, Keystone, Corporate Pro
  • 5 to 20 years, established: Beacon, Clearwater, Pacific
  • Under 5 years, growing: Vivo, Crest, Apex

3. What vibe do you want?

  • Traditional, trustworthy: Corporate Pro, Heritage
  • Premium, substantial: Beacon, Keystone
  • Modern, approachable: Clearwater, Pacific, Vivo
  • Distinct, creative: Crest, Apex

4. What color family fits your brand?

  • Navy / deep blue: Beacon, Keystone, Heritage
  • Blue / teal: Clearwater, Pacific, Heritage
  • Purple / indigo: Crest
  • Charcoal / cyan: Apex
  • Teal / orange: Vivo
  • Neutral / corporate: Corporate Pro
