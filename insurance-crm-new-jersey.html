NJ AGENT FAQ

Questions New Jersey Agents Ask Us

Does the CRM handle New Jersey's mandatory auto coverage requirements?

Yes. Quote forms can be configured to capture NJ-specific coverage fields including PIP, bodily injury, and property damage so your quote prep starts with the right information.

Can this work for agents serving both NJ and the NYC metro market?

Yes. The CRM and automations are licensing-agnostic at the tool level. You control which state's products you sell. Many of our NJ clients write cross-border policies and use the same pipeline.

Is this built for high-density, high-volume urban markets?

Yes. Speed-to-lead automation and missed-call text-back are especially valuable in dense markets where leads shop multiple agents within minutes.
