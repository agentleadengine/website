WHY SMS FOR INSURANCE

Email Goes To Promotions. SMS Gets Read.

98 Percent Open Rate

SMS gets opened within 3 minutes on average. Email averages 20 percent open, mostly hours or days later.

45 Percent Reply Rate

Insurance quote follow up texts get replies at 45 percent vs 6 percent for email. Two way conversations close deals.

Lower CPL

Reactivating a lead via SMS costs less than half of phone dialing. Pair SMS with dialer for full coverage.

USE CASES

Every Insurance SMS Workflow, Automated

01

New Lead Instant Text

Lead fills form, SMS fires in under 60 seconds. No more 18 hour response windows. Higher contact rate than any other channel.

02

Missed Call Text Back

Someone calls, you are with a client. Auto SMS fires: "Missed your call, can I text you?" Recovers 30 to 50 percent of missed inbound.

03

Quote Follow Up Cadence

5 text sequence after quote delivery: day 1, 3, 7, 14, 30. Each text has a clear CTA and opt out language.

04

Renewal Reminders

60, 30, 14, 7 day pre renewal texts. Clients know the date is coming, you stay top of mind before competitor quotes arrive.

05

Claims Status

Claim opened, adjuster assigned, check cut, closed. Each status change can auto text the client. Retention through claim is 3x higher.

06

Review Generation

Day 7 after bind, happy client gets a review ask text. Drives Google reviews organically without bribes.
