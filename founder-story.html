FOUNDER STORY

Why We Built Agent Lead Engine.

Insurance agents are extraordinary at policy. They are mediocre at marketing software, and that is not their fault. Agent Lead Engine exists to close that gap.

THE PROBLEM

What Kept Showing Up

Every independent insurance agency we talked to had a version of the same story. Leads coming in from Facebook, lead vendors, referrals, walk ins. Producers trying to work them in between policy renewals, carrier calls, and claim service. A patchwork of Excel sheets, sticky notes, missed voicemails, and wishful thinking.

The tools that promised to solve this were either built for tech startups and felt alien to an insurance agent, or built for captive call centers and priced for enterprises. Nothing sat in the middle for the independent agent writing $3M to $50M in premium.

Meanwhile the captive carriers were running national speed to lead programs that the independents could not touch. Five minute response windows, dedicated SDRs, AI call scoring. The gap was widening every year.

THE BUILD

What We Decided to Make

Insurance First

Not a generic CRM bolted onto insurance. Every workflow, field, pipeline built for policy lifecycles from day one.

Under 14 Day Launch

Long implementation kills momentum. Snapshot deploys in days, not months.

Month to Month

If we have to lock you in to keep you, we are doing it wrong.

Honest Gaps

We will tell you what we cannot do before you sign. Painful surprises are the fastest way to lose trust.

Affordable for Independents

Pricing that a 2 to 10 person agency can justify, not enterprise retainers.

Human Onboarding

Real people on kickoff calls, not video libraries and chatbots.

WHAT WE BELIEVE

The Beliefs Underneath

Speed to lead matters more than nurture length. A 60 second text beats a 6 email drip.

Retention beats acquisition. One saved policy is worth 5 new leads.

Compliance is not a checkbox. TCPA, 10DLC, CAN-SPAM enforcement is tightening, not loosening.

The client owns the data. Always exportable, always theirs, always ready to hand back.

Small agencies deserve enterprise tools at independent agency prices.
